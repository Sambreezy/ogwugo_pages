Customer Care

Ogwugo is dedicated to providing you with the best possible shopping experience. We operate in a company built on trust and guest satisfaction. This can only be achieved through communication and experienced support. Contact our Customer Service team to provide a comment or ask a question about our local store or our corporate headquarter.

If there's a problem with a transaction, we'll put a hold on the funds until the issue is resolved. We investigate and stay involved every step of the way. Our customer service are geared to making sure you receive what you ordered.
