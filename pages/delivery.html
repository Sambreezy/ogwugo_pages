Order, tracking and delivery information

You've placed your order and now are anxiously awaiting your finished product. You can
view tracking information for your shipment by logging in to your Blurb account, going to the My
Orders page then clicking the order number.

From there, you can view information about your order including the delivery-by date, destination,
and tracking information.

If your order has shipped and there's no link to a tracking number, this means it shipped via a non-
trackable method. (Most shipping methods are trackable, and methods that aren't trackable will
show non-trackable next to the available delivery dates at check-out).

Having an issue with delivery? Please contact our Customer Care. Include your order number in
your email so that we can more quickly resolve your issue.
